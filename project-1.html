Project Overview

This is a browser game, where you move a ball through a maze to get to the green block. After finishing one level you can click on button in the top right corner to get to the next level. The structure of the maze is randomly generated for every new level. For the physics and movement I used the matter.js library, besides that no additional library or framework.

Hosted on Netlify.
